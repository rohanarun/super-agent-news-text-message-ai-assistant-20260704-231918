How a text becomes real computer action

1) You text

Plain English like “open Twitter on device 2” or “run the marketing play on all devices.”

2) Super routes

Your message is routed to the right live device or cloud sandbox tied to your account.

3) Computer use

Super clicks, types, scrolls, and navigates like a human would.

4) Cache & replay

Successful actions are saved and replayed from the computer-use cache for repeatable workflows.
